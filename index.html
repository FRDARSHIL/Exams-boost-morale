🧠 Smart Study Tips

📅

Plan Your Schedule

Break your syllabus into daily chunks. Small wins every day add up to big success.

💤

Sleep Well

Your brain consolidates memory during sleep. 7–8 hours a night keeps your mind sharp.

🥗

Eat Right

Fuel your brain with healthy food. Avoid heavy meals before studying.

🧘

Take Breaks

Use the Pomodoro technique — 25 minutes of focus, then a 5-minute break.

✍️

Active Recall

Test yourself instead of just re-reading. Writing and recalling boosts retention.

🤝

Study Groups

Explaining concepts to peers deepens your own understanding.
